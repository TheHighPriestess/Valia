Mejor by V > About Me

My name is Valia Iosifidou and I've been working as a hair stylist for 26 years. I opened my own salon, Mejor by V, 16 years ago. It's located in the Pagrati neighborhood of Athens, Greece.

Whether you're a more alternative type person interested in playing around with color and a funky cut or somone just looking for a great, simple, classic style you'll feel welcome because I do my best to take into account your own personal wants and lifestyle. All ages are welcome, too.

Athens' expat community had provided me with many loyal customers and I'm proud to say that they're a very diverse group and from all over the world. So, if your Greek is a bit rusty, don't worry. We speak English.

I love what I do and I consider myself a perfectionist. I love to travel and I keep up with the lastest techiques via seminars in London, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, and Greece. I've also worked with magazines and well-known personalities in Greece.

So, if you're looking for a talented, competent, professional, English-speaking stylist in Athens please don't hesitate to call and make an appointment. Consultations are free, of course!
